About Me

Sitanshu Shekhar is a digital marketing expert with over 5 years of experience in the industry. He has a strong educational background in Computer Science from the University of London, which he blends with his marketing expertise to create effective strategies for companies across the globe.

His core strengths lie in various aspects of digital marketing, including SEO, SEO copywriting, digital branding, and UX. He has worked with a diverse range of companies, including Dahalconsulting, Eproperty Nepal, Discovery World Trekking, Coalition technologies, Thames International College, Tck Tck, and many others.

He has also founded his own companies, Hiddenlayer LLC in the US and Hiddenlayer Network Pvt Ltd in Nepal, both of which provide innovative digital marketing solutions to clients worldwide.
